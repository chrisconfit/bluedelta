How it works

The Blue Delta Jean Builder & Closet helps you build, save, share and order your custom jeans. Keep track of all saved and ordered jeans in your own personal closet.

Each pair of Blue Delta Jeans is customized to your body and you will work closely with a Blue Delta Representative to fit and personalize your jeans. Our customizer is designed to give you a representation of your jeans so you can start deciding on your style, fabric, and thread colors before we walk you through the rest of the process.

Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions about using the customizer or ordering your jeans - info@bluedeltajeans.com | 888.963.7879

Ordering

We have a couple of ways that you can be fitted for your Blue Delta Jeans. Remember, if you are an existing member of the Blue Delta Jeans Family, you can always re-order a pair of jeans in your closet, of use a previous pair of jeans as your fit for a new pair.

Get fitted in person

You can get fitted at our flagship studio in Oxford, Mississippi, or from a number of cities at partner tailors. After selecting your jean for order, you'll be able to choose the city for your fitting.

Clone Jeans

Not able to book a fitting in person? Do you already have a perfect fitting pair of jeans?Blue Delta Jeans can CLONE your perfect fitting pair of jeans. Send us your jeans and we can use the measurements to create your unique pattern.

Please note, due to the custom nature of our jeans do not charge any money until you have been fitted. A Blue Delta Jean Representative will be in touch within 24 hours of your order inquiry being placed to discuss personalization options and pricing.
