How it works

We have a couple of ways for you to buy a pair of Blue Delta Jeans. If you are an existing member of the Blue Delta Jeans Family, you can always re-order a pair of jeans in your closet, of use a previous pair of jeans as your fit for a new pair.

Bespoke

From $500

Choose from our full selection of fabrics, threads, and styles to create your bespoke jean. With the help of a Blue Delta Jeans tailor, you can design your perfect jean.

  • Get fitted by a BDJ Stylist or one of our partner tailors
  • Choose from our full selection of 30+ fabrics
  • Design your jean using our full range of thread options
  • A personal pattern that is hand-drafted and kept on file for easy re-order through your BDJ online closet
  • Personalized consultation with Blue Delta stylist to design your bespoke jeans
  • Add on options available - rivets, custom pocket bags, monogramming, and more
  • Lifetime Alterations and Repairs
Start Building

Fit Match

From $300

For those lucky few that have the perfect pair of jeans, we can match your fit! Choose from a limited selection of raw denim fabrics to design your fit match jean.

  • Mail in your favorite jeans and we will match the fit
  • Choose from our 8 Fit Match fabrics
  • Design your jean using our full range of thread options
  • A personal pattern that is hand-drafted and kept on file for easy re-order through your BDJ online closet
  • Personalized consultation with Blue Delta stylist to design your bespoke jeans
  • Add on options available - rivets, custom pocket bags, monogramming, and more
  • Lifetime Alterations and Repairs
Try Fit Match

Please note, due to the custom nature of our bespoke jeans, we do not charge any money until your order details have been confirmed. A Blue Delta Jean Representative will be in touch within 24 hours of your order inquiry being placed to discuss personalization options and pricing.
